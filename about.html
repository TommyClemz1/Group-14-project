Welcome to Group 14 News website, a student-led news platform created by a team of dedicated students from Lagos State University (LASU). Our goal is to provide a centralized hub for news and information relevant to the LASU community. Currently, our news section redirects to the official LASU website, where you can find the latest updates and news from the university. As we continue to develop and grow, we plan to expand our content to include original articles, features, and analysis on topics that matter to LASU students. We're excited to have you join us on this journey and look forward to bringing you fresh perspectives and insights from the LASU community. Stay tuned for updates and new content!
